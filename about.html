About TPAO Petroleum Company

A trusted energy partner committed to responsible petroleum development and national energy security

Who We Are

TPAO Petroleum Company is a state-oriented petroleum organization with decades of experience in oil and gas exploration, drilling, production, and energy infrastructure development. Our operations span onshore and offshore fields, supported by modern engineering practices and internationally recognized safety standards.

We work closely with national and international partners to ensure reliable energy supply while maintaining strict compliance with environmental and regulatory frameworks.

Our Mission

Our mission is to explore, produce, and manage petroleum resources efficiently and responsibly, contributing to national economic growth and long-term energy independence.

We are committed to operational excellence, workforce development, and the adoption of innovative technologies that enhance productivity while minimizing environmental impact.

Our Vision

To become a globally competitive petroleum company recognized for technical expertise, transparent governance, and sustainable energy practices.

We aim to strengthen our position in regional and international energy markets by investing in research, digital transformation, and human capital development.

Operational Excellence

TPAO Petroleum applies internationally accepted engineering standards across all operations. From seismic surveys to production facilities, our processes prioritize safety, efficiency, and long-term asset integrity.

Continuous improvement, risk management, and performance monitoring are central to our operational philosophy.

Health, Safety & Environment (HSE)

Health, safety, and environmental protection are core values at TPAO Petroleum. We maintain strict safety protocols, environmental monitoring systems, and emergency preparedness programs across all project sites.

Our goal is zero harm to people, communities, and the environment while delivering sustainable energy solutions.

Core Values

  • Integrity, transparency, and accountability
  • Safety-first operational culture
  • Innovation and technical excellence
  • Environmental responsibility
  • Professional workforce development
